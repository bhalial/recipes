Ingredients:

- 2 vegan sausages
- 1 cup of mushrooms, sliced
- 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 can of baked beans
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 4 slices of bread
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional extras: vegan bacon, scrambled tofu, roasted potatoes
- Instructions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add the vegan sausages and cook until browned on all sides.

2. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook until they are tender and lightly browned. Remove the sausages and mushrooms from the pan and set them aside.

3. In the same pan, add the halved cherry tomatoes and cook them until they are slightly soft and juicy.

4. Heat up the baked beans in a small saucepan over medium heat.

5. Toast the bread and slice the avocado.

6. To assemble the English breakfast, place the toast on a plate and top with the cooked sausages, mushrooms, cherry tomatoes, and baked beans. Add the sliced avocado on the side.

7. Season everything with salt and pepper to taste.

8. Optional extras: serve with vegan bacon, scrambled tofu, or roasted potatoes on the side.

9. Serve immediately and enjoy your vegan English breakfast!
