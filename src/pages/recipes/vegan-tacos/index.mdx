Ingredients:

- 8-10 corn tortillas
- 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 medium-sized sweet potato, peeled and cubed
- 1/2 red onion, sliced
- 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional toppings: avocado, salsa, lime wedges, cilantro, shredded lettuce
- Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. Toss the sweet potato, red onion, and red bell pepper with olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

3. Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender and slightly crispy.

4. While the vegetables are cooking, heat the black beans in a small saucepan over medium heat.

5. Warm the tortillas in the oven for a few minutes or in a skillet over medium heat.

6. To assemble the tacos, spoon some black beans onto each tortilla and top with the roasted vegetables. Add any desired toppings, such as avocado, salsa, lime wedges, cilantro, or shredded lettuce.

7. Serve immediately and enjoy your vegan tacos!
