Ingredients:

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 4-5 potatoes, peeled and chopped into small pieces
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond or soy milk)
- Optional toppings: chopped chives, vegan sour cream, or crumbled vegan bacon bits

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and saut√© until the onion is soft and translucent.

2. Add the chopped potatoes to the pot and stir to combine with the onion and garlic.

3. Pour in the vegetable broth and add the dried thyme. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are soft and fully cooked.

4. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth, or transfer the soup to a blender and blend in batches until smooth.

5. Stir in the non-dairy milk and continue to cook the soup over low heat for an additional 5-10 minutes, until heated through.

6. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

7. Optional: garnish the soup with chopped chives, vegan sour cream, or crumbled vegan bacon bits.

8. Serve your vegan potato soup hot and enjoy!
